Ruby embarked on the path of yoga in 2017 in an effort to ease her depression and health issues. It worked! Since then, the practice has been an ally and sacred ritual to ground her to the present moment. Her mission is to share the tools of yoga to promote holistic healing on and off the mat. She encourages functional movement, strength training, play, breathwork, meditation, myofascial release, and creative expression.

While working as an elementary school teacher, Ruby had a dream to continue teaching, but from her mat instead of the classroom. The dream came true when she went to Peru and got certified in 2023, specializing in vinyasa yoga. She loves traveling, gardening, art, outdoor activities, reading, writing, and spending time with her dog. Join her for mindful movement practices that seek to harmonize the body, mind, and spirit.
